Adjust Your Posture

Please take a moment to adjust your posture. Here are some tips to help you:

  • Keep your feet flat on the ground.
  • Align your back with the back of your chair to help maintain good posture.
  • Relax your shoulders; they should not be rounded or hunched.
  • Ensure that your ears are in line with your shoulders.
  • Take regular breaks to stand up, stretch, and move around.
